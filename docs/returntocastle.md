🎮 Fun & Gameplay:
very confusing gameplay, entities are visible through walls and it's not clear what the player is supposed to do
tab key is supposed to open the map, but it doesn't work and there's no indication of how to open the map
power ups are spawned inside walls and are not obtainable, and there's no indication of what they do or how to use them
strafe with q and e is very unintuitive, especially since the game doesn't indicate that you can strafe at all
shooting is very glitchy, bullets move toward the center of the screen from a gun that is located at the bottom of the screen, and it's not clear where the bullets are coming from or how to aim them
after rescuing all the civilians, the game respawns more enemy targets, thus the game can never be won and there's no end goal or victory condition. the vending machines don't seem to be interactable. the random events don't make much sense.

🎨 Graphics & Audio:
very repetitive music, but there's music at least!
emoticons are cute, but the art style is pretty basic and not very cohesive. The UI is pretty rough and could use some polish.
very tiny font size on certain parts of the title menu
very common font
intelligence briefing lore text is not very readable, the first part is cut off. same for field manual tutorial and field roster screens: very crowded and hard to read
leaderboard is scrolling very slow and is not very readable
enemy chat text is very pixelated and unreadable

🤖 AI Implementation:
It looks like a completely vibe coded game, which is impressive per se. but a human polished version of this game would be much more enjoyable to play.

💡 Final Thoughts:
nice idea of a wolfenstein 3d, doom style game with an office/bureaucracy theme, but the execution is very rough and the game is not very enjoyable to play in its current state. with some polish and refinement, this game could be a lot of fun to play.
